Activities

Bike Tour

Bring your friends and family and book a tour with Johnny. Johnny is the perfect guide. Not only does he love cycling, he has also lived in Nelson for many years and knows all the secrets Nelson has to offer. We know that a good cycle fit can make or break your tour, so each bike is fitted to each cyclist's unique size. Your bike comes with a comfortable saddle, a rear rack and a lockable compartment. Johnny offers two routes on his tours, one that goes outside of the city to Golden Bay with stunning views of the coastline. This for experienced riders and takes a full day.

For the less hardy, he offers a short, easy bike around around Nelson's arty boutique shops and cafes, to the Nelson Cathedral and to the wearable Arts Museum. This takes one to two hours.

Vineyard Tour

Join you cellar Master and Vineyard Host Jimmy Brown for a tour our vineyard and winemaking processes, barrel education and the history of the Marlborough wine industry.

You can see traditional winemaking practices and hear about the history of winemaking. Experience the art of viticulture and winemaking from grape to the bottle at five different vineyards. Nelson is a boutique wine area and is famous for Pinot Noir, Chardonnay and Sauvignon Blanc. You will sample the very best at the many wineries on your tour.

Movie night and Games Evening

At our hostel we have movie nights in summer. There is something truly special about having a movie outdoors under the stars. Every Wednesday and Saturday evening, we have a special movies in the Garden night. If it rains, we still have movies under the pavilion. We have a 2mx2m size screen and stereo surround sound. The names of the movies are posted on the hostel notice board each week.

In the hostel you can also play card games with your friends in the evening. We will and a card game competition, the winner can get free beer!
