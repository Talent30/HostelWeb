Welcome to Nelson hostel

Come and stay at one of the best motels in Hamilton. Auto Lodge Motel is located only minutes north of central Hamilton, away from the central traffic, easy to find with ample parking. Close to Supermarkets, Cafes, Sky City Casino, Waikato Stadium, Water World, Claudelands Event Centre and the Base Shopping Centre.

We offer accommodation to suit all needs; from Studio suites, One-bedroom Suites to Spa Bath Studio Suites. Our Spa Bath Suite, including a luxurious spa, is perfect for a quiet wind-down or a weekend getaway. All units are fully equipped for a comfortable and relaxing stay whatever the occasion.

We also offer all the modern day necessities in all our suites including 42 inch Flat Screen Televisions with +50 Sky Digital Channels, Free Unlimited fibre Wifi access, Heat pump and Air-conditioner, large parking area and much more.

Only 30 minutes from the Hamilton Airport and 90 minutes from Auckland International Airport.
We look forward to seeing you at our motel.
