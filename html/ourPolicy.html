Our Policy

Welcome to Nelson hostel


When the reservation process has been completed, rooms are held for your exclusive occupation in our computerised reservation system. The nature of our business requires us to apply a strict policy for cancellations and is as stated below;

Non-refundable Cancellation Policy



- Any booking cancellations will result in a cancellation fee of the full accommodation fee.

- Any booking modifications may result with additional fees and charges.

24 Hour Cancellation Policy


- Any Cancellations made before 2pm on the day prior to the check in date will result to no cancellation fees.

- Any Cancellations made after 2pm on the day prior to the check in date will result to a cancellation of the full accommodation fee.

- Any Booking modifications may result with additional fees and charges.

Please note, Special Cancellation terms and conditions may apply to Group Bookings or long term bookings.
We reserve the right to accept reservations that are subject to a minimum stay period over peak periods. A request to reduce the length of stay on an existing application may result in the reservation being cancelled.
